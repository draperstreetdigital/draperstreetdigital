How it works

Four steps, start to live

No jargon, no long contracts. You'll know exactly what's happening at each stage.

  1. 01

    Walkthrough

    We sit down — in person or on a call — and look at your business, your products, and what you're already selling face to face.

  2. 02

    Build

    We build your actual storefront: product pages, checkout, shipping or local pickup, and a layout that works as well on a phone as it does on a laptop.

  3. 03

    Launch

    We connect payments, test every order path end to end, and go live together — with a real order run through the system before customers ever see it.

  4. 04

    Handoff or ongoing

    We either teach you and your team to run it day to day, or manage it for you on a simple monthly retainer. Your call.
